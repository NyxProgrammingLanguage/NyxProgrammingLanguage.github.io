Legal Grounding & Protection Framework
4.1 — Common Law & Passing Off

Common law trademark rights arise automatically upon first use in commerce. The NYX™ marks are protected under the passing off doctrine — three elements are established:

GoodwillNYX™ has acquired goodwill through public release, active use, and 144+ user community adoption since 2026.
MisrepresentationAny third party using a confusingly similar name (NyxLang, NyxOS, NyxCloud, etc.) for competing software would misrepresent its origin to developers.
DamageSuch misrepresentation would damage the goodwill and commercial interests of Surya Sekhar Roy.
